About Madrasa‑e Anwar ul Uloom • Sundarapalya

A tradition of Ilm & Hifz since 2000

Madrasa‑e Anwar ul Uloom was established in 2000 by the Mumineen (people) of Sundarapalya. Today it is regarded as one of the finest madrasas in KGF Taluk, known for nurturing Qur'an memorisation (Hifz) and classical Islamic learning.

Our Mission

To provide students with a strong foundation in the Qur'an, Sunnah, and Arabic—cultivating character, discipline, and service to the community. The madrasa's growth is a reflection of the dedication of the people of Sundarapalya and the guidance of its esteemed teachers.

From its inception in 2000, the madrasa has thrived through the duas, efforts, and contributions of the local community. This bond keeps the institute vibrant and future‑focused.
